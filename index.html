Meet Marcia Medrano, DVM

Dr. Medrano is originally from Miami, Florida. She graduated with honors from St. George's University and completed her clinical year at the University of Pennsylvania. Prior to relocating to sunny California, she completed an internship at a busy specialty hospital in NYC.

Dr. Medrano loves being a house call veterinarian. She enjoys getting to know each of her patients and cares for them as if they are her own.

In her spare time Dr. Medrano enjoys hiking, live music, painting, and traveling with her husband and kitty, Schmitty the Siamese mix.
